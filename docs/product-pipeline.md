# Product Pipeline

## Ultimate Product Goal

Build VerdeAI into an AI-powered, end-to-end datacenter management platform covering discovery, switch operations, configuration governance, power efficiency, and forecasting-driven capacity planning.

## Strategic Pipeline

1. AI Discovery And Device Intelligence
- Build continuous discovery across network and compute assets.
- Deliver confidence-scored asset identification and topology sync.

2. Switch Configuration Health Checks
- Implement vendor-aware configuration validation.
- Surface health/risk posture and remediation suggestions.

3. Configuration Audits And Compliance
- Deliver immutable audit trails for all infrastructure changes.
- Enforce policy controls and compliance reporting.

4. Power Efficiency Optimization
- Integrate power and thermal telemetry.
- Optimize workload placement for energy and carbon efficiency.

5. Intelligent Forecasting Of Resource Requirements
- Forecast compute/network/storage capacity demand.
- Proactively warn on projected exhaustion and cost risk.

## Cross-Cutting Enterprise Requirements

- Full RBAC and approval workflows
- Multi-tenant security isolation
- SSO and SCIM
- Full observability and auditability
- High-availability control plane
